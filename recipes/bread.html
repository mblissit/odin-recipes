Bread

The first test for all young Gielinorian chefs

Many a young chef have tried their hand at this Gielinor staple. One must only take a step onto Tutorial Island to smell the hundreds of burnt loaves keenly crafted by our young protagonists. But fear not! I will teach you how to not only avoid burning your bread, but to make a beautiful golden loaf.

Lets begin our cooking adventure!

Ingredients

  • Pot of flour
  • Bucket of water

Directions

  1. Start by combining your two ingredients to form a nice bread dough. It should simply look like some uncooked dough.
  2. Immediately place the bread dough in a range to cook. It is of the utmost importance that you do not neglect to remove your bread from the range properly and timely. If I might give any advice on how to achieve a good result I would suggest a few fondles of the range, that will do just great.

Well done! You've embarked on your first adventure on the journey to becoming a master chef! If you keep this up you'll be joining me at the Cooks' Guild before long!
